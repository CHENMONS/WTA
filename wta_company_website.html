Who We Are

PT Widya Techno Abadi is a leading construction company based in Medan, specializing in boiler construction and installation services. We provide comprehensive EPC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction) and operational procurement construction services, with special focus on the palm oil mill industry.

Our company is committed to delivering dedication to manufacturing industries, especially palm oil mills, to improve performance and quality standards across Indonesia.
